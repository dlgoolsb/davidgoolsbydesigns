Where Music Meets Comedy...

Jokez N Jamz was started in July of 2017 in Greensboro, North Carolina by rapper, Antion Scales, and comedian, David Goolsby. The two launched the concept as a showcase that highlighted both musical and comedic talent from all over the state. A few months later, Roger Gore II, better known as DJ Gito, joined the team on a quest for a creative uprising in the area. They began to regularly host the event at Artist Bloc in Greensboro as a monthly show.



In August of 2018, Jokez N Jamz hosted its biggest event as a part of Welcome Week at North Carolina A&T State University in Corbett Sports Center. After the show, the trio separated for an unspecified hiatus to further their individual careers and to this day still remain close friends.

Our Mission

There are two primary missions behind Jokez N Jamz:

To provide a platform for independent creatives seeking opportunities to grow their audience and expand their network.

To create a safe and fun environment for people who want to enjoy and discover talented artists in their area.
